A&T Carwash App

Background:

After working at A&T, a local family-owned car wash business, I considered how to streamline the service. In the area, A&T is well-known and long running, yet it could benefit from introducing more modern innovations. While the machinery and washing equipment was up to standard, the service and ordering process would certainly benefit from improvement. Typically cars wait in line as a worker takes their order, marking the car with the desired services accordingly. While some customers enjoyed this face to face interaction, there would always be others who might prefer a digital ordering experience. This is where I came up with the idea of an A&T Carwash App prototype, designed simply to make the ordering experience as effortless as possible.

Goal:

Develop an app prototype which allows customers to order a carwash service without needing to roll down the window, thus freeing up time for workers to focus on other tasks.

Role:

UX Designer
